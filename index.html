About Us

We are a restaurant that specialises in providing an exceptional seafood dining experience for our seafood lovers.We started as a business that sold home-made cooked fish to the people of Umlazi, a township located in Durban,South Africa.We would set-up a stand near the Umlazi Z section taxi rank and sell to commuters travelling by taxi to work.This lead to us growing into a successful SME that employs 156 people from all-over the Durban region.We are currently located in Durban,South along the beach promenade next to steers.
